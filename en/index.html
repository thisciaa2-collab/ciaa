Biography

Ciaa "EL DURO", formerly known as ThisIsCris, is the creative pseudonym of Cristóbal Ignacio A, an up-and-coming songwriter and artist based in Chile who is breaking ground in the global urban music landscape.

Armed with sharp delivery and heavy trap-infused beats, he officially debuted with the hit single "Sin Destino Fijo" in May 2026. The track originated organically during studio sessions, turning into a viral phenomenon and showcasing his potential on an international scale.
